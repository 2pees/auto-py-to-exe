Auto Py to Exe

Auto Py to Exe easily converts python scripts to executable files.

Simply select your script location and choose other settings that are given in the GUI. When finished click the big blue convert button and wait while the output is displayed.

The output will say when it is complete and you will be able to clear the output if you want to make a small change and re-convert.
